Welcome to Accent Press!

Since we've had a lot of people tell us that they can't figure out how to use it, we've made this page to help!

  1. Open the settings, and choose which languages you want. This tells the extension which accents to enable or not.
  2. Select the speed. The higher the speed, the more time it takes for the accents to switch (We're working on something more intuitive, don't worry!)
